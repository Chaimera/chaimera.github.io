About Us.

An appealing Kolkata-based cafeteria presents unique varieties of chai perfectly brewed with high quality tea leaves and indigenous components, along with a hint of surprise. Catering to the taste buds of all the tea lovers of Kolkata, we prioritise at instilling a sense of belonging in our customers towards their favourite "Chaa", while savouring the experience with every sip. It is time, that one can customize his/her own cup of tea and experience the sense of euphoria.

Chai Mera brings forth a perfect blend of exciting flavours and ingredients just like the "Chimera"-- showcasing diverse species in a single organism. Thus, creating a refreshing delight. How can one forget the infamous obsession towards "Chaa"? The first thing we need in the morning while turning the newspaper pages? Chai Mera has got you covered in every possible way, providing with new yet unique combinations of food elements. Chai Mera is all set to change the stereotypical way of consuming chai.

What is more interesting than a subscription scheme which is solely designed to satisfy the daily cravings of office employees and business men? With advancements in technology and services, we deliver your favourite "chaa" at your workplace. Our goal to provide our people with the finest quality of tea is, achieved by obtaining hand-picked tea leaves from the premium plantation of Jorhat Valley and the beautiful valleys of Darjeeling, Guwahati & Shillong. Our supremely efficient team of top-notch professional tea testers are not just testing it themselves but are conducting tea tours for people all over Kolkata.
